What is AiHum...

AiHum (AI + humanity) is an independent Artificial Intelligence (AI) and data science group with a great software backbone that seeks to bridge the gap between AI and the Humanities.

The principles of AiHum

we are committed to creating projects that are ethical, responsible, and beneficial to humanity. We strive to ensure that our projects are designed with the utmost respect for human rights and dignity. We also recognize the importance of diversity in our work and strive to create an inclusive environment where everyone can contribute their unique perspectives and skills.

what is our mission...

Our mission is to create projects that use AI and data science to benefit humanity and provide exceptional educational opportunities for people of in different levels. We believe that AI should be used to improve the lives of people, not just as a tool for profit or power.

We invite you to join us!

We believe that by combining our expertise in AI with our commitment to human rights and dignity we can make a real difference in the world. If you have the same concern and the necessary skills, we invite you to join us on this journey!
